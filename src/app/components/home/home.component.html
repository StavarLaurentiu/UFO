How to Play the UFO Battle Game

  1. Register/Login: If you want to save your highscores and compete in the global ledearboard you can create an account or login if you already have one, using the buttons in the right corner.
  2. Set Your Preferences: Before starting, you can customize your game by choosing how many UFOs to battle (from 1 to 5) and how long the game will last (60, 120, or 180 seconds). If you are logged in you can choose to save your preferences locally or remote.
  3. Start Playing: After setting your preferences, select "Play." UFOs will appear on the screen, moving randomly across the screen, and you will control a missile launcher at the bottom.
  4. Control the Missile: Use your keyboard to move the missile left or right, and press the spacebar to fire at the UFOs.
  5. Score Points: Each UFO hit earns you 100 points. You can only fire one missile at a time, and you will need to wait for it to either hit a UFO or disappear off the screen before shooting again.
  6. Store your highscores: If you are logged in you can save your highscores to the server by selecting the "Record Score" option.

Game Rules

  • One Missile at a Time: You can only have one active missile. If you miss all UFOs, your score will drop by 25 points.
  • Game Over: The game ends when your chosen time runs out. Your final score will be adjusted:
    • Your score is divided based on the game length (no reduction for 60 seconds, divided by 2 for 120 seconds, and by 3 for 180 seconds).
    • For each extra UFO (more than one), 50 points are deducted. For example, with 5 UFOs, 200 points will be subtracted.
  • High Scores: You can view the top 10 scores on the server by selecting the "Records" option. You can register or log in to save your scores.
